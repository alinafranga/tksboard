VIEWS

The total number of times a retailer visited the product details page for one your products on Producer Connect in the selected time period. Each View is weighted by the retailer’s number of store locations at the time of the interaction.

LIST ADDS

The total number of List Adds your products received in the selected time period. Each time one of your products is added to a list belonging to a retailer on Producer Connect this counts as a List Add. Each List Add is weighted by the retailer’s number of store locations at the time of the interaction.

Total Follows
